Hawa Mahal, the Palace of Winds was built using pink sandstone in 1799 by Maharaja Sawai Pratap Singh to facilitate ladies of the royal household to enjoy processions and observe crowd in the below street without being noticed by the people and is the most recognizable monument of Jaipur. This unique five-storey structure with small latticed windows (called jharokhas) is a blend of Hindu and Islamic architecture.
